My name is Alison Xianting Huang, or Hinting Wong (Cantonese pronunciation). I am an incoming Ph.D. student in CS at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), I will be joining the MISC Lab, supervised by Prof. Irwin King (ACM, IEEE Fellow). Prior to this, I completed my B.Eng. at Jinan University, where I worked with Prof. Ke Wang in the NexGenAI Lab.

My future research will explore topics in Trustworthy AI, Rec Sys, LLM-based Learning, FinTech, etc. Feel free to reach me out for collaboration or just a chat! Any guidance is really appreciated.
